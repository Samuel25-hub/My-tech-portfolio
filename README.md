# Project: How to Make the Perfect Cup of Coffee
*A Quick Start Guide for Beginners*

## Prerequisites
* Fresh coffee beans
* A grinder

## Instructions
1. **Grind the beans:** Set your grinder to "Medium."
2. **Prepare the filter:** Rinse with hot water to remove paper taste.
3. **Brew:** Pour water in a circular motion over the grounds.

> **Note:** Never use boiling water directly on grounds to avoid scorching.
