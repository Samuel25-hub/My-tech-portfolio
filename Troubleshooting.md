## Troubleshooting Guide: Wireless Headphones

| Symptom | Possible Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **No Sound** | Headset is powered off | Press and hold the **Power** button for 3 seconds. |
| **Audio Lag** | Signal interference | Move away from microwave ovens or high-power Wi-Fi routers. |
| **Blinking Red Light** | Low battery | Connect the USB-C cable to a power source for at least 20 minutes. |
| **Cannot Pair** | Device is in "Sleep" mode | Toggle Bluetooth **OFF** and **ON** on your smartphone settings. |
